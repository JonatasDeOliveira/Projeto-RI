2006-03-23 00:01:00 Information by Roman Sol
I invite everyone to take part in Open Contest 2006! The competition starts on March 23 at 00:01 (SPOJ time) and ends on April 15 at 00:01 (SPOJ time). The scoring system is arranged in such a way that the score received for a solution strongly depends on the best solution to the problem (see rules for more details). The long duration of the competition should give you the necessary time to think thoroughly about each of the problems and to find the optimal solution.

Good luck to all participants of the competition!
