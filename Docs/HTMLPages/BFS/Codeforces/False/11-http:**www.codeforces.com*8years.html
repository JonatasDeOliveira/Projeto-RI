Most recently, Codeforces has celebrated its 8th birthday — a binary jubilee, the 1000th anniversary! I hope that my idea has worked and the result of the efforts has become an important part of the world of programming contests. I look enthusiastically at the future of the platform and I hope that the Codeforces team will be able to please with innovations, improvements and rounds over and over again.

Friends, we will be glad and grateful to a gift from you, your contribution to the future of Codeforces. Your donations will allow us to move forward and implement ideas. I appeal to all those who are not indifferent to the fate of Codeforces for support. Your help will not only give us the resources to work and develop, but will also inspire us as an affirmation of the demand for our efforts.

We need resources to pay rewards for problem writers and coordinators, development, operations and hardware upgrade. Work on Codeforces never stops. Please read the report on the results of 2017. One more year we have not slowed down, are full of enthusiasm and we are glad to see the growing audience of Codeforces.

For 8 years of work, Codeforces has helped many programmers learn more, gain new skills. I am sure that we can do even more in our common forces.

Thank you,
Founder and CEO, Codeforces
Mike Mirzayanov
