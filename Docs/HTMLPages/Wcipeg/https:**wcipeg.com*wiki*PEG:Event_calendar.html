Note: Dates listed for ACSL contests 1-4 are the deadlines for marks to be submitted.
The actual contests are usually held a couple of weeks before the dates listed below.

October[edit]

  • 18th: COCI Contest 1
  • 27th: Beaver Computing Challenge - Registration Deadline

November[edit]

  • 6th/7th: Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge
  • 8th: COCI Contest 2
  • Week of 10th (any 45 minute period): Beaver Computing Challenge
  • 29th: COCI Contest 3

December[edit]

  • 19th: ACSL Contest 1
  • 20th: COCI Contest 4

January[edit]

  • 17th: COCI Contest 5

February[edit]

  • 5th: Canadian Computing Competition - Registration Deadline
  • 7th: COCI Contest 6
  • 10th: Pascal, Cayley, and Fermat Contests - Registration Deadline
  • 13th: ACSL Contest 2
  • 18th: Canadian Computing Competition (CCC)
  • 24th: Pascal, Cayley, and Fermat Contests

March[edit]

  • 7th: COCI Contest 7
  • 13th: ACSL Contest 3
  • 20th: Fryer, Galois, and Hypatia Contests - Registration Deadline
  • 20th: Euclid Contest - Registration Deadline
  • 25th - April 1st: ECOO Boardwide Programming Contest (Round 1)
  • 28th: Croatian Olympiad in Informatics

April[edit]

  • 15th: Euclid Contest
  • 16th: Fryer, Galois, and Hypatia Contests
  • 17th: ACSL Contest 4
  • 25th: ECOO Regional Programming Contest (Round 2)

May[edit]

  •  ??: Asia-Pacific Informatics Olympiad in Indonesia
  • 9th: ECOO Final Programming Contest (Round 3)
  • 11th - 15th: Canadian Computing Olympiad (CCO)
  • 23rd: ACSL All-Star Contest in Orlando, FL

July[edit]

  • 19th - 26th: 27th International Olympiad in Informatics in Almaty, Kazakhstan
