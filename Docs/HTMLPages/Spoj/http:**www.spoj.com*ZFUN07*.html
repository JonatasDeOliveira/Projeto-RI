2007-04-04 12:36:39 Announcement by Roman Sol

I invite everyone to take part in Open Contest 2007! Competition starts on April, 15th at 00-01 (SPOJ time) and ends on April, 27th at 00-01 (SPOJ time). Competition will go on in the best traditions of DASM league. Interesting problems, original scoring system and long carrying out time will make this competition very dynamical and interesting. The scoring system is arranged in such a way that the score received for a problem strongly depends on the best solution for this problem, in more detail about this system you can read in rules. Long duration of competition will give necessary time to think thoroughly above each of problems and to find the optimal solution.

Good luck to all participants of competition!
